About us

Our story

Created in 2nd of july 1927 by its founder CMB, Toboloc always aimed to provide entertainment to everyone everywhere around the world, with high quality and secured slides and water slides. Now managed by Jean-Baptiste Capella (alias “Trinity power”) and Yohan Villiers (alias “yoyo53”), the company still continues on the same tracks as its founder, always searching for innovative and hightech slides.
